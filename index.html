About Our Clinic

A New Standard in Therapeutic Bodywork

At Neuro Massage Therapy, we are building a clinic where specialized therapists work together under one roof, united by a commitment to precision and lasting results. Our approach goes beyond relaxation — we focus on restoring function, relieving chronic pain, and supporting the long-term health of every client we serve.

Through precise, targeted bodywork, we help restore natural function, improve range of motion, and support long-term mobility and longevity. Every session is purposeful, structured, and tailored to the individual.

Based on the St. John Method of Neuromuscular Therapy, we treat a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions — from chronic neck pain, TMJ dysfunction, headaches and migraines, to shoulder pain, lower back pain, hip pain, sciatica, plantar fasciitis and referred pain throughout the body. If you are experiencing pain or discomfort anywhere in your body, chances are we can help.

We treat the body as a unified whole — because true healing begins when we address the root cause, not just where it hurts.

What We Offer

Our Specialized Treatments

Each modality we offer is carefully selected for its ability to produce real, measurable results in pain relief, function, and recovery.

01

Neuromuscular Therapy

NMT · Trigger Points

A specialized, hands-on approach that brings balance to the nervous and musculoskeletal systems. We focus on the true source of dysfunction — identifying and releasing trigger points, decompressing nerves, and restoring the body's natural movement patterns for lasting relief.

02

Cupping Therapy

Myofascial Decompression

Using the decompression technique, cupping lifts and separates the fascia from the underlying layers of skin and tissue. This creates space within the soft tissue, promoting circulation, releasing adhesions, and accelerating the body's natural healing process — a powerful complement to neuromuscular therapy.

03

Dolphin Neurostim

Microcurrent Point Stimulation

The Dolphin Neurostim applies concentrated microcurrent stimulation to specific points on the body, combining the principles of acupuncture, osteopathy, and neurology. It works by calming the nervous system, releasing chronic muscle tension, and reducing scar tissue — providing fast, lasting pain relief without needles or medication.
